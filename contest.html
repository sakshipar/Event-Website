Flaunt your creative scarecrows on window display at your favorite STC businesses and stand a chance to win $500!!

Changes made to the contest

Some changes are coming to the Scarecrow Contest, and we need you to help keep this family-friendly tradition fun and safe.
Scarecrow Stroll 2020 is being modified to work within Illinois Phase 4 guidelines for Covid-19.
The Scarecrows will be placed throughout downtown and paired with businesses.
Contest rules can be found here.

Prize Details

All entries must be submitted before 4:30 p.m. on September 18, 2020.
Mechanical & Mega Category - Open to all
1st place prize - $500
Individuals Scarecrow Category - Open to all
1st place prize - $500
Clubs, Not-for-profits & Schools
1st place prize - $500
Business Scarecrow Category - Open to all Businesses
1st place prize - $500

How to Vote

This year there will be online voting on the St. Charles Business Alliance Facebook page.
The Scarecrow photo with the most likes will win this years Scarecrow Stroll Contest!
Voting begins on October 9th - 11th

Check out the St Charles Business Alliance Facebook to vote! Thank you to St Charles Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, Ram
