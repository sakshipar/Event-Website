Drive-In Concert

Kick off the weekend with a Drive In Concert by the Four C Notes on Thursday October 8th from 6:30pm-8:00pm at James O. Breen Community Park. Tickers are $5.00 per car and can be purchased here.
More information on the concert can be found here.

Scavenger Hunt

Check out all the specials going on around town during the Scarecrow Stroll October 9-11. Once you visit as many businesses as you can, check them off on this form. Complete the front & back side of the scavenger hunt and turn it back into the Information Booth during Scarecrow Stroll for a chance to win up to $50 in St. Charles gift cards!

Pumpkin Carver

The professional pumpkin carver will be located in front of the St Charles History Museum at 215 E Main St, St. Charles, IL 60174 on Saturday Oct. 10th from 10am-5pm. Be sure to stop in the history museum for all sorts of St Charles themed gifts and their newest exhibit. STCAlliance Professional Pumpkin Carver is all set up at the St. Charles History Museum until 5pm! Stop by and grab a free pumpkin from @intowneselfstorage !

Photography Station

Stop by our photo opportunity and capture a family photo! The photo opportunity will be available October 9th-11th . Pick one of the three displays for a fun fall photo. Come take a fall family photo at the St. Charles Scarecrow Stroll photography station! The photography station will be located in the BMO Harris parking lot October 9th -11th.

Craft Beer Box (Adults)

There will also be items individuals can purchase and take home with them from the fest.
Adults can enjoy a local Craft Beer Box, which will feature seasonal craft brews from all St. Charles breweries. Preorder your St Charles Craft Beer Box today! The Craft Beer Box includes a fall beer from the 6 local breweries in St. Charles. The Craft Beer Box is $50.00 and will be available to pick up on Friday, October 9 3pm-7pm at D & G Brewing Co. & Saturday, October 10 3pm-6pm at Pollyanna Brewing Co. All proceeds help support St. Charles Breakfast Rotary Club. Thank you to our sponsor Griffin Williams LLP. Pre order your St Charles Craft Beer Box at www.scarecrowfest.com/craftbeerbox

Scarecrow in a box

Provides materials to make a scarecrow at home.
Scarecrow in a Box will be sold for $5.00 a box.
Each box will include panty hose, twine, an outfit, straw to stuff the scarecrow and directions.
Make sure to stop by and purchase your Scarecrow in a Box on Friday from 12pm-6pm, Saturday 10am-6pm and Sunday 10am-6pm. Scarecrow in a Box will take the place of the childre''s favorite Make Your Own Scarecrow this year during the St. Charles Scarecrow Stroll! You will be able to purchase and bring home all the materials needed to make your own scarecrow right at home! Thank you to our sponsor McNally's Heating and Cooling! @brianmcnally12

Jazz

Stay the whole weekend during Jazz Weekend and sip on local cider at the Sunset Cider Stroll. During St. Charles Jazz & Art Weekend, Fox Valley Jazz Big Band will be performing at James O. Breen Community Park for a Drive In Concert in partnership with the St. Charles Park District! Thank you to MetroNet Fiber for making the Drive In Concert possible. Purchase Tickets here: www.stcjazzweekend.com/event-details
